# Cab-Travel-Behaviour-Trends
Analyzed urban cab travel data to identify peak hours, high-demand locations, and trip patterns. Derived insights to improve route optimization and reduce waiting time. Supported operational planning using travel behavior trends.Tools Used: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, SQL, Power BI
Abstract
Urban travel patterns provide important insight into how cab services are used across different locations and time periods within a city. This project focuses on understanding cab travel behavior in urban areas by analyzing how cab services are used across different locations and at different times of the day. The main objective is to identify peak travel hours, frequently visited locations, and the overall movement pattern of trips within the city. By examining these travel trends, it becomes easier to understand which areas experience higher demand and which time periods require greater service attention. This also helps in identifying important routes that contribute most to daily travel activity. The study provides a clear view of how travel demand changes based on location and time, which can support better planning of cab availability and route management. It also helps in improving service efficiency by reducing waiting time and ensuring that travel services are aligned with customer movement patterns. Overall, the analysis offers useful insights into urban mobility and highlights how travel behavior can influence operational planning and service improvement
